Our Background

A New Era of Mental Health Care in Anambra State

Solution Rehabilitation Centre (SRC) is a flagship mental health initiative of the Anambra State Government, located in Nibo, Anambra South Senatorial District. Commissioned in 2026, the centre was established to address the critical gap in accessible, high-quality psychiatric and substance use rehabilitation services in the South-East geopolitical zone of Nigeria.

With an 80-bed capacity and a multidisciplinary team of 60 trained professionals, SRC is equipped to provide comprehensive inpatient and outpatient care for individuals living with substance use disorders, psychiatric conditions, and related neuropsychiatric illnesses.

The centre is supervised by the Anambra State Ministry of Health and operates in compliance with the standards of the Medical and Dental Council of Nigeria, the Nursing and Midwifery Council of Nigeria, and the National Drug Law Enforcement Agency (NDLEA).

Our Treatment Framework

The Biopsychosocial Model of Care

First described by Dr. George Engel, the Biopsychosocial Model is the globally recognized gold standard for psychiatric and substance use disorder treatment. It recognizes that illness arises from a complex interaction of biological, psychological, and social factors — and that lasting recovery requires addressing all three simultaneously.

01

Biological Dimension

We address the neurochemical, genetic, and physiological underpinnings of mental illness and addiction through:

  • Comprehensive psychiatric and medical evaluation on admission
  • Supervised medical and drug detoxification
  • Evidence-based pharmacotherapy for all conditions
  • Laboratory investigations and physical health monitoring
  • Management of co-occurring medical conditions
  • Nutritional assessment and dietary rehabilitation
  • Sleep hygiene and physical wellness programs
  • Seizure disorder management and anticonvulsant therapy
02

Psychological Dimension

We explore the emotional, cognitive, and behavioural aspects of each patient's condition through structured therapy:

  • Individual psychotherapy (CBT, Motivational Interviewing, DBT)
  • Group psychotherapy and peer support sessions
  • Family therapy and systemic intervention
  • Psychoeducation for patients and caregivers
  • Trauma-informed care
  • Relapse prevention and coping skills training
  • Cognitive rehabilitation for neurocognitive impairment
  • Dialectical Behaviour Therapy (DBT)
03

Social Dimension

We rebuild the patient's social world, functional roles, and community connections through comprehensive rehabilitation:

  • Psychosocial rehabilitation and daily living skills training
  • Vocational and occupational rehabilitation
  • Family engagement and caregiver support programs
  • Community reintegration planning
  • Social welfare assessment and linkage to support services
  • Stigma reduction and mental health advocacy
  • Aftercare, follow-up, and alumni peer networks
  • Community outreach programs

Conditions We Treat

Psychiatric & Neuropsychiatric Conditions

💊

Substance Use Disorders

Cannabis, opioids, sedatives, stimulants, and polysubstance use disorders — including supervised detoxification and long-term rehabilitation.

🧩

Psychosis & Schizophrenia

Acute psychotic episodes, chronic schizophrenia, schizoaffective disorder, and substance-induced psychosis — managed with evidence-based antipsychotic therapy and psychosocial support.

Epilepsy and other seizure conditions — including those associated with substance withdrawal and substance-related neurological complications.

🔁

Dementia & Cognitive Disorders

Assessment and management of dementia, mild cognitive impairment, and substance-related neurocognitive disorders, including caregiver education and support.

🔬

Dual Diagnosis

Co-occurring substance use and psychiatric disorders (dual diagnosis) — requiring integrated treatment that addresses both conditions simultaneously.

Who We Admit

Admission Criteria

Solution Rehabilitation Centre accepts patients experiencing any of the following conditions and who would benefit from inpatient or outpatient psychiatric and rehabilitation services:

✓ Substance use disorders (cannabis, opioids, and other substances)
✓ Acute psychiatric conditions requiring stabilization (psychosis, mania, severe depression)
✓ Dual diagnosis — co-occurring substance use and psychiatric disorders
✓ Chronic psychiatric conditions requiring long-term rehabilitation
✓ Anxiety disorders requiring structured inpatient therapy
✓ Seizure disorders associated with substance use or neuropsychiatric conditions
✓ Dementia and neurocognitive conditions requiring specialist management
✓ Patients referred by general practitioners, hospitals, or specialist clinicians

How It Works

Step-by-Step Admission Process

1

Initial Contact

Send us a WhatsApp message, or complete the online enquiry form. Our trained intake team will speak with you confidentially and help determine the most appropriate level of care.

2

Pre-Admission Assessment

A brief clinical assessment will be conducted by phone or in person to understand the patient's presenting condition, treatment history, and immediate needs — to determine the appropriate ward, treatment plan, and level of care.

3

Admission Day

On the day of admission, the patient will be received by a member of the nursing team, undergo a comprehensive medical and psychiatric evaluation, have laboratory investigations conducted, and be assigned to the appropriate ward under the care of a consultant psychiatrist.

4

Treatment Planning

Within 48 hours of admission, the multidisciplinary team will develop an individualized treatment plan addressing the biological, psychological, and social dimensions of the patient's condition, with clear goals and timelines for each intervention.

5

Treatment & Rehabilitation

The patient engages in the structured treatment program — including pharmacotherapy, psychotherapy, group therapy, skills training, and social rehabilitation — under continuous clinical supervision.

6

Discharge Planning & Aftercare

Discharge is carefully planned in collaboration with the patient, family, and treatment team. An aftercare plan is developed, including follow-up appointments, community support linkage, and relapse prevention strategies to sustain recovery.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does treatment typically last? ▾
Treatment duration varies depending on the nature and severity of the condition. Detoxification typically takes 7–14 days, while a full rehabilitation program may last 4–12 weeks. The treatment team will discuss an individualized timeline with the patient and family during the admission process.
Is the centre government-funded? Are there fees? ▾
Solution Rehabilitation Centre is a government-owned facility commissioned by the Anambra State Government. Details on service fees, subsidies, and payment plans can be obtained by contacting the Administration Department directly via our WhatsApp helpline.
Can family members visit patients during admission? ▾
Yes. Family involvement is an important part of the treatment process. Structured family visits are scheduled regularly, and family therapy sessions are offered as part of the treatment program. The clinical team will advise on visiting arrangements based on the patient's treatment plan.
Is patient information kept confidential? ▾
Yes. All patient information is handled with strict confidentiality in accordance with the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) and the ethical standards of the Medical and Dental Council of Nigeria. Patient information will not be shared without explicit consent, except where required by law.
Do you offer outpatient services? ▾
Yes. In addition to inpatient services, the centre offers outpatient consultations, follow-up appointments, and aftercare programs for patients who have completed inpatient treatment or who require ongoing community-based support.
What should a patient bring on the day of admission? ▾
Patients should bring valid identification, any current medication or medical records, personal clothing and toiletries for the expected length of stay, and the contact information of a next of kin. Our intake team will provide a full checklist on confirmation of admission.
